Please place your pet images and music in this folder:

Required files:
- OIIAIOIIIAI.gif (animated GIF for active state)
- OIIAIOIIIAI_stop.png (static PNG for idle state)

You can use any pet images you like - cats, dogs, birds, or any other creature!
