Please place your pet images and music in this folder:

Required files (Group 1):
- OIIAIOIIIAI.gif (Group 1 animated GIF)
- OIIAIOIIIAI_stop.png (Group 1 static PNG)
- oiia-oiia-sound.mp3 (Group 1 background music)

Required files (Group 2):
- OIIAIOIIIAI2.gif (Group 2 animated GIF)
- OIIAIOIIIAI2_stop.png (Group 2 static PNG)
- oiia-oiia-sound2.mp3 (Group 2 background music)

Each group has its own GIF, PNG, and music file.
The application can switch between groups, and within each group, toggle between GIF and PNG.

Recommended image size: 180x180 pixels or smaller (to fit within the 200x200 window with 10px margin)
Supported music formats: MP3, OGG, WAV

You can use any pet images you like - cats, dogs, birds, or any other creature!
